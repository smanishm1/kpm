About Us

Kraft Paper Mill LLC is a premier kraft paper manufacturer delivering high-quality, eco-friendly solutions across the United States. As a sister company and manufacturing partner of Ariya Packaging Industries (API), we combine global know-how with local service to support your growth while meeting rigorous environmental standards.

From our Norristown, PA facility, we provide responsive lead times, consistent quality, and flexible order quantities—whether you need kraft reels, sheets, specialty grades, or corrugated packaging components.

Our Strengths & Capabilities

  • Advanced, automated lines delivering > 25,000 MT per year.
  • FSC-aligned sourcing and resource-efficient processes.
  • Custom GSM, widths, cores, and finishes on request.
  • Regional logistics for PA, NJ, NY, and beyond.
  • Application support from packaging specialists.
  • Consistent strength and porosity control.
  • Greaseproof/wet-strength options for specialty needs.
  • Strict QA checkpoints from pulp to finished roll/sheet.
  • Short runs to bulk volumes with reliable schedules.
  • Private-label and print options for retail presentation.
